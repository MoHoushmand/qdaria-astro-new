## The Quantum Threat is Real

As quantum computing advances, the cryptographic algorithms that currently secure the world's digital infrastructure—RSA, ECC, and others—face an existential threat. Shor's algorithm, running on a sufficiently powerful quantum computer, will be able to break these encryption methods, exposing sensitive data to anyone who has harvested it.

This is not a distant "what if." It is a "when." This scenario, known as "Harvest Now, Decrypt Later," means that data stolen today can be decrypted tomorrow.
