### 2.0a Compensation Methodology (Median + N% Anchoring)

Each pre-seed base salary equals the Norwegian median for the role tier, multiplied by `(1 + N/100)`, where `N` is the percentage above median assigned to that individual:

- **+12% over median**: Lillian Kristiansen, Sharareh M. Shariat Panahi
- **+8% over median**: John Kristiansen
- **+5% over median**: all other employees (Caroline Woie, Rajesh Chavan, Lindsay Sanner, Gaspar Alvarado, Nick Saaf, Yulia Ginzburg, Nils Bjelland Gronvold, Fredrik Krey Stubberud, Daria S. Houshmand)

The CEO base is set independently at 160,000 EUR (founder-set, not derived from the median formula).

Norwegian medians sourced from `salary-seed.ts` `norwegianMedianSalaries`: ceo 145K, coo 100K, senior 88K, mid 75K, junior 45K. Values are 2025 Norwegian-market data.

Examples:
- Lillian (Senior, +12%): 88,000 × 1.12 = 98,560
- John (Senior, +8%): 88,000 × 1.08 = 95,040
- Caroline (Senior, +5%): 88,000 × 1.05 = 92,400
- Sharareh (COO, +12%): 100,000 × 1.12 = 112,000
- Gaspar (Mid, +5%): 75,000 × 1.05 = 78,750
- Daria (Junior, +5%): 45,000 × 1.05 = 47,250

The factors that determine each individual's `N` (education, prior experience, demonstrated contribution to QDaria, tenure, domain expertise, market benchmarking, role criticality, funding stage at offer, geographic anchor, performance history, board approval at thresholds) are listed in Section 2.5 of every employment contract.

Seed through IPO progression preserves the per-tier growth multipliers used by the prior schedule, just re-anchored to the new pre-seed base. Senior tier growth multiplier = 2.262 (Pre-Seed to IPO); Mid = 2.159; Junior = 2.180; COO = 2.777; CEO = 2.500. All values rounded to nearest 500 EUR.
